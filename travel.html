Travel for International Guests

Default route: VIE Airport, then bus or train to Trenčín, with car travel option.

flight_takeoff Step 1 - Fly to Vienna International Airport (VIE)

For most international guests, the easiest entry point is Vienna International Airport (VIE).

directions_bus Step 2A - Bus option (default first check)

Check direct buses from VIE to Trenčín on FlixBus first. If timings do not match your flight, use a coach via Bratislava as fallback.

  • FlixBus: Vienna Airport (VIE) - Trenčín
  • Slovak Lines: Vienna Airport - Bratislava coach service

train Step 2B - Train option (common rail approach)

Start at Flughafen Wien (airport rail station), connect through Vienna (usually Wien Hbf or Wien Mitte, depending on your connection), then continue toward Slovakia and onward to Trenčín.

  • ÖBB journey planner
  • ÖBB official website
  • Vienna Airport rail connectivity (S-Bahn/Railjet)

directions_car Step 3 - Car travel

For guests who prefer full flexibility, renting a car from VIE is a practical direct option to reach the wedding area.
