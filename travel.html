Events & Things to Do

For guests staying longer — festivals, castle visits, and Trenčín 2026 highlights.

festival Pohoda Festival

July 8–11, 2026 — Slovakia's biggest music festival, two weeks before the wedding. 30th anniversary edition with The Cure headlining. Held at Trenčín Airport.

  • Pohoda Festival — tickets & info

castle Trenčín Castle

One of the largest castles in Central Europe, overlooking Trenčín. Summer opening hours (May–Sept) typically 9:00–18:45. Hosts exhibitions and cultural events. In 2026, The Well of Love exhibition runs until late August.

  • Trenčín Castle — visitor info

celebration Trenčín 2026 — European Capital of Culture

Trenčín is European Capital of Culture for 2026. Year-round program of concerts, exhibitions, theatre, and more. Check the official calendar for events around the wedding dates.

  • Trenčín 2026 — events calendar

event Beckov Castle events (day trip)

Beckov Castle is about 20 km from Trenčín and regularly hosts themed historical events. If you are staying a bit longer, this is an easy and popular day trip from the city.

  • Visit Trenčín — Beckov Castle overview
  • Beckov Castle — event calendar

Travel for International Guests

Default route: VIE Airport, then bus or train to Trenčín, with car travel option.

flight_takeoff Step 1 - Fly to Vienna International Airport (VIE)

For most international guests, the easiest entry point is Vienna International Airport (VIE).

directions_bus Step 2A - Bus option

Check direct buses from VIE to Trenčín on FlixBus first. If timings do not match your flight, use a coach via Bratislava as fallback.

  • FlixBus: Vienna Airport (VIE) - Trenčín
  • Slovak Lines: Vienna Airport - Bratislava coach service

train Step 2B - Train option

Start at Flughafen Wien (airport rail station), connect through Vienna (usually Wien Hbf or Wien Mitte, depending on your connection), then continue toward Slovakia and onward to Trenčín.

  • ÖBB journey planner
  • ÖBB official website
  • Vienna Airport rail connectivity (S-Bahn/Railjet)

directions_car Step 3 - Car travel

For guests who prefer full flexibility, renting a car from VIE is a practical direct option to reach the wedding area.
