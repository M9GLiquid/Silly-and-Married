Travel for International Guests

Default route: VIE Airport, then bus or train to Trenčín, with car travel option.

For most international guests, the easiest entry point is Vienna International Airport (VIE).

  • Good onward connections to Trenčín by bus or train.
  • Car rental is available at the airport if you prefer to drive.
  • Typical total transfer time to Trenčín: about 2-3 hours.

Check direct buses from VIE to Trenčín first; this is often the simplest option.

  • Typical journey time: about 2-3 hours, depending on connections.
  • If schedules do not match your flight, use a coach via Bratislava as fallback.

Useful links

  • FlixBus: Vienna Airport (VIE) - Trenčín
  • Slovak Lines: Vienna Airport - Bratislava coach service

Start at Flughafen Wien (airport rail station), connect through Vienna, then continue toward Slovakia and onward to Trenčín.

  • Most common transfer stations: Wien Hbf or Wien Mitte.
  • Use the ÖBB planner to confirm live connections and book.

Useful links

  • ÖBB journey planner
  • ÖBB official website
  • Vienna Airport rail connectivity (S-Bahn/Railjet)

For full flexibility, renting a car from VIE is a practical option.

  • Drive time to the wedding area: about 1.5-2 hours.
  • For motorway travel, plan for two digital vignettes (Austria + Slovakia), usually about €18-24 total depending on validity.
  • Cross-border driving is usually possible, but declare Austria to Slovakia at pickup; rules vary by company and vehicle class.

Useful links

  • Vienna Airport official car rental list (VIE)
  • Austria official vignette (ASFINAG)
  • Slovakia official vignette (eZnamka)
